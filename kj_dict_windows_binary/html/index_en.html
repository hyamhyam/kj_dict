Free Korean-Japanese dictionary "KJ_dict"

KJ_dict is very simple Korean-Japanese dictionary.
          

feature

  • incremental search by Hiragana, Kanji, Hangul
  • incremental search by Roma-ji
  • Free (GPL)
  • Hangul IME is included .
